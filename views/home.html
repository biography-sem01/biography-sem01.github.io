2022 Nobel Prize laureates in discussion

Join Nobel Prize laureates as they engage in a lively discussion about their award-winning discoveries and achievements, and explore the potential practical applications of their work. The thought-provoking conversation is moderated by Zeinab Badawi of the BBC in "Nobel Minds."

2022 Nobel Prizes: List of Winners

The 2022 Nobel Prize winners were announced, honoring individuals who have made exceptional contributions to their respective fields. The Nobel Prizes, which are awarded annually in six categories - Physics, Chemistry, Medicine, Literature, Peace, and Economics - recognize individuals who have advanced our understanding of the world and made a positive impact on society.
